Lasagna Recipe

Hearty, rich, cheesy and classy beef lasagna that's perfect for family dinners.Get ready to prepare this perfect cozy meal.

Ingredients:

For the meat sauce:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500g ground beef
  • 800g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the bechamel sauce:

  • 50g butter
  • 50g all-purpose flour
  • 500ml warm milk
  • Pinch of nutmeg
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Other:

  • 9 lasagna noodles(pre-cooked or oven-ready)
  • 2 cups shredded mozarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. Make the Meat sauce:
    1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
    2. Add onion and cook until soft, about 5 minutes.
    3. Stir in garlic, and then add the ground beef and cook until browned.
    4. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, pepper and sugar.
    5. Simmer uncovered for 20-30 minutes until thickened.
  2. Make the Bechamel sauce:
    1. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.
    2. Stir in flour and cook for 1-2 minutes.
    3. Gradually whisk in warm milk until smooth.
    4. Cook while stirring until thickened.
    5. Add nutmeg, salt, and Parmesan cheese. Remove from heat
  3. Assemble the Lasagna:
    1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. In a baking dish, spread alyaer of meat sauce.
    3. Add a layer of noodles, then bechamel sauce, and repeat layers until used up.
    4. Top with mozzarella.
  4. Bake:
    1. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
    2. Uncover and bake for another 15-20 minutes until bubbling and golden.
    3. Let rest for 10 minutes beore slicing.
