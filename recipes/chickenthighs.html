Honey Garlic CHicken Thighs

Honey Garlic Chicken Thighsare a sweet and savory dish with juicy chicken cooked in a flavorful honey garlic sauce. Quick and easy, it's perfect for a weeknight dinner!

Ingredients:

For the chicken:

  • 6 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs (or boneless, skinless if preferred)
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil or vegetable oil

For the sauce:

  • 1/3 cup honey
  • ¼ cup soy sauce (low sodium preferred)
  • 3-4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar or apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil (optional for extra flavor)
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp cornstarch + 1 tbsp water (for thickening, optional)

Instructions:

  1. Season the Chicken
    • Pat chicken dry with paper towels.
    • Season both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Sear the Chicken
    • Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    • Place chicken thighs skin-side down and cook until golden and crispy (5-7 minutes).
    • Flip and cook the other side for 3-4 minutes. Remove and set aside.
  3. Make the Sauce
    • In the same pan, reduce heat to medium.
    • Add garlic and sauté for 30 seconds until fragrant.
    • Add honey, soy sauce, vinegar, sesame oil, and pepper.
    • Stir and bring to a simmer.
  4. Simmer the Chicken
    • Return chicken to Pan.
    • Spoon Sauce over the thighs.
    • Cover and simmer for 20-25 minutes, turning halfway, until chicken is cooked through (internal temp: 165°F / 74°C).
  5. Optional-Thicken Sauce
    • Mix cornstarch and water in a small bowl.
    • Stir into the sauce and simmer for 2-3 minutes until thickened.
